## 2. Exit-gate evidence

- **The REQ-016 × REQ-163 interlock holds under synthetic load (the IRD exit condition):** `QualityController.test.ts` drives the controller through hand-constructed and organically-simulated frame-time sequences. `applyTier` is proven to never drop a `critical: true` item regardless of tier or budget (including a budget of zero); the controller degrades exactly one rung per pressured, cooldown-elapsed tick (never skips); scale-up requires a genuinely CONSECUTIVE run of `stabilityWindowTicks` good ticks, with a single interrupting bad tick proven to reset the streak to zero rather than merely pause it; the shared `cooldownTicks` floor is proven to block a second transition from firing immediately after the first, which is what actually prevents boundary oscillation (the hysteresis gap alone would not, at tick-by-tick cadence, if the EMA hovered exactly between thresholds).
- **Grammar integrity (REQ-070/071):** `Grammar.test.ts`'s strict parser structurally rejects unknown categories, unbound roles, double bindings, and non-critical danger; `StylePack.test.ts`'s `validatePack` structurally rejects a pack that shares an accent across categories, gives optimization a chromatic accent, reuses a motion class, or repeats an audio patch — REQ-070's "mixing signatures strictly prohibited" is enforced by construction, not by review, on both the grammar side and the pack side.
- **Style-swappability (dm-0076):** no module outside `render/style/paper/` names a Paper Collage material; the grammar, scene compiler, executor, and quality controller are provably pack-blind — they consume only the `StylePack`/`VisualGrammar` seam types, never a concrete colour/shape/texture literal (`RenderIsolation.test.ts` plus each subtree's own structural tests).
- **Projection purity (dm-0004/dm-0082):** `GameShell.test.ts` proves the shell's fixed-step sim advance is BIT-IDENTICAL to a headless `Engine`/`StateManager`/`CANONICAL_PIPELINE` run fed the same `InputFrame`s — rendering reads state one-way and never feeds simulation, held even through the full shell (camera, scene compilation, audio cues, feel modifiers, Poki lifecycle) stacked on top.
- **Isolation (dm-0081), confirmed by the scan and by git:** nothing under `src/` or `tools/` imports `render/`; within `render/`, only `render/tooling/` imports `tools/` (the editor/overlay/inspector reuse, zero P8 module modified); `render/` reaches `src/eval/` only through `AgentHarness`'s `CANONICAL_PIPELINE`, never `eval/local/`, `eval/gdos/`, `eval/campaign/`, or gen/ internals; browser globals (`document`/`window`/`WebGL2RenderingContext`/`AudioContext`/`PokiSDK`/etc.) and wall-clock reads (`Date.now`/`performance.now`) appear nowhere under `render/` outside a `platform/` prefix that was never created this phase (dm-0103 — deliberately deferred to P11, since no real browser/build pipeline exists to honestly verify bindings against yet); `Math.random` appears nowhere in `render/`.
- **Determinism:** cue derivation, scene compilation, batching, pooling, viewport math, the quality controller, and pack rasterization command streams are all proven pure/repeat-run-identical across the phase's test suites — no unthreaded real time anywhere; the one real-time-adjacent input (`Clock`'s `realDeltaSeconds`, the shell's `advanceFrame` argument) only ever decides HOW MANY already-deterministic fixed steps run, never their content.
- **Package policy (dm-0038/dm-0086):** P9 added **zero new npm dependencies** — raw WebGL2/WebAudio/Canvas2D via `lib: ["DOM"]` type-only usage plus hand-rolled `Fake`/trace-recording devices for every seam (`Raster2D`, `Gl2Device`, `AudioDevice`, `AssetFetcher`, `PortalSdk`); the Poki SDK integrates via its documented CDN-script lifecycle contract (`PortalSdk`/`NullPortalSdk`), never an installed package.
- **No content creep:** every level fixture across all eleven slices is test-scoped scaffolding (grid fixtures, blank editor drafts, the S9.8 shell fixtures); there is no `levels/` directory; P10 stays closed through P9's close.
